Welcome to Toke's Agroenterprises

At Toke's Agroenterprises, we believe in the transformative power of aquaculture and sustainable agriculture to drive positive change in our community. Our mission is to empower women by providing them with the skills, resources, and opportunities needed to excel in the aquaculture industry and beyond.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to foster economic independence and create sustainable livelihoods for women through comprehensive training and support in aquaculture and agriculture. We are committed to promoting food security, environmental sustainability, and community well-being.

  • Our Vission

    We envision a future where women are at the forefront of sustainable aquaculture and agricultural practices, leading their communities with innovation, resilience, and empowerment. Our goal is to be a beacon of change, inspiring women to realize their full potential and contribute to a thriving, self-sufficient community.

Our Approach

We offer a range of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of women in our community, including hands-on training in aquaculture techniques, sustainable agriculture practices, and business development workshops. Our approach is centered on practical learning, community support, and a commitment to creating lasting change.
